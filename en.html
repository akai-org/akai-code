AKAI Code

The first edition of the AKAI Code hackathon organized by AKAI - Academic Web Application Research Group - takes place at the Poznań University of Technology.

The idea of an event is simple! You code an application related to the topic within 24 hours, and then the jury decides which team will win.

There are three categories to compete in - Best Idea, Best Prototype and Best Technology Usage. What's the topic? You'll find out soon!

Topic

The theme of the first edition of AKAI Code is "The Social Influence".

Introduce us to an idea that solves a problem of a small or or larger community that you're part of.
Have an idea on how to support your local food bank? nice! Know how to help an animal shelter in your area? Great! Have you come up with a solution to old lady Jadzia's problem? Let's go!
Find your jumble, take it apart, figure out the solution and at the end shine in front of a jury!

Categories

Best Idea

The most interesting initiative that solves in an interesting and real way the problem of Your community.

Best Prototype

The best implementation of an idea, it means the most accurate way to execute an idea.

Best Technology Usage

The best technical execution of the project - means the best choice of technology, programming, or clever software solutions.
